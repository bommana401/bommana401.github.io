Purpose:

The purpose is to create a user-friendly platform that encourages users to discover the potential of renewable energy. Recognizing that the realm of clean energy can sometimes appear overwhelming, hindered by technical terms and an excess of information. This website has been designed with simplicity and clarity at its core, ensuring that renewable energy concepts become comprehensible to readers.

Key Features:

Simplified & Informative Content:

Website offers simplified explanations of solar and biogas energy, discussing topics such as their mechanisms and benefits.

Energy Calculations:

The platform has a energy calculator tool allows users to estimate the electricity and gas production based on solar panels and biodigesters calculated in different surface areas. With prebuilt Columbus, OH weather data, users can visualize the potential energy generated from renewable sources tailored to your specific location.
