Solar Energy and BioGas Caculator

Instructions:

  1. Fill out the questions in Monthly Energy Consumption section to get personalized energy estimates.
  2. Look up your residence in the map searchbar.
    Measure the area you want to panels or digestors placed. Once you do, click "Calculate Panel" or "Caculate Digestor"
  3. To add another surface, then click "Measure new surface". Once you measure, click either "Add to solar surface area" or "Add to BioGas surface area". The list of the surfaces should be diaplayed to the left.
  4. To remove a surface either from Biogas or solar, click on the buttons to the left. Input the position of the surface you would like to remove.
  5. Scroll down to results to see the energy produced by all the surface you have selected.
  6. Click on the "Show Annual Savings" in either Solar or BioGas Results to see the annual savings with the current renewable energy produced.
  7. When you make changes the Solar Panel option or measure new surface, click "Show Annual Saving" button, to see the updated results. Similarily, when you remove a surface, click on "Calculate Panels/Biodigestor" to see the updated results.
  8. Simply refresh window to restart again.
